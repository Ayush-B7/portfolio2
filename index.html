#3: PCI Infographic

This project seeks to present the effectiveness of Percutaneous Coronary Intervention (PCI) as a treatment for Artheroschlerosis and Heart Disease in a manner that may be readily understood by the general public. Due to the inaccessibility of public-facing and credible medical research or clinical data, a succinct yet accurate summary of the mainstream analysis of this complicated procedure has proven to be immensely beneficial to patients. As such, this infographic illustrates the benefits, limitations and significant risks associated with the procedure in order to better inform and protect patients and their families.

#7: Conference Presentation

This project seeks to inform the public on the comprehensive clinical data supporting the use of Coronary Artery Stents as a treatment for a variety of cardiovascular conditions. To this end, the project presents the underlying biomechanics that govern blood flow, shear stress on arterial walls and the methodology surrounding clinical research of coronary stenting. Furthermore, the project summarizes the consensus opinion of the medical community by drawing from credible, peer-reviewed journals and extensive clincial testing.
